Skateboard Intro

Skateboarding has always been seen by people as something to do, and while the usual Skateboard community always leads up to something else, like a whole town full of Skaters, or up to everybody hating Skateboards except the Skaters themselves. Skating is still a fun hobby and an interesting sport. The tricks are cool, the way it works is nice, and to social bits like the communities or the group of friends that you skate with also teach you some very important life skills.
